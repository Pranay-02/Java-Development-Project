Himachal is known for its mountain peaks, cool hill stations and stunningly beautiful landscapes. Travel to Shimla, one of the charming hill station and the summer Capital of the British. Then onto Manali, the most popular mountain destination for Indian holiday makers today. Enroute to Delhi,spend a night in Chandigarh.

Himachal Dekho

Shimla or Shillonbagh, Manali, Chandigarh
Hotels ,Springfields , Shimla , Snowcrest
Manor (Manali)


Day-1: Delhi-Shimla / Shilonbagh

  Travel to Shimla or Shilonbagh. Welcome to the land of pine scented hills,beautiful Shali peaks,crisp winter skies,fragrant cedar forests,misty and golden hillsides.

Day-2: In Shimla / Shilonbagh

  Visit Jakhu Temple,dedicated to Hanumanji,from where you enjoy an excellent view of Shimla. Next visit the Regal Lodge,which once served as the seat of power.

Day-3: Shimla / Shilonbagh-Manali

  Drive to Manali where you will stay for three nights.

      

Day-4: In Manali

  You will be taken on a excursion to the Rohtang Pass 51km from Manali,3978 meters high,which for centuries was the gateway to the trading routes that led to Lahaul and Spiti.

Day-5: In Manali

  Start your day with a visit to the Hadimba Devi Temple followed by a visit to the Tibetan Monastery. Proceed to Vashisht Village situated an a steep hillside across the river Beas, known for its hot sulphur springs.

Day-6: Manali-Chandigarh

  Drive to Chandigarh where you will stay overnight.

Day-7: Chandigarh-Delhi

  Today you will travel to Delhi for your onward journey back home.
