Day-1: Trivandrum-Aquaserene.

Arrive Trivandraum and journey to Aqauserene along lush green paddyfields and coconut groves dotted with busy hamlets.Reach Aqaserene where the scene is idyllic and tyhe mood-romantic


             

Day-2 : In Aquaserene

Spend the day leisurely at your resort enjoying tthe facilities. For the ultimate experience rejuvenate your body and soul with an Ayurvedic massage.

Day-3 : Aquaserene-Kumarakom

In the morning drive to Kumarakom where your resort promises you the experience of a lifetime,a tryst with the soul of Kerala.Spend the afternoon in the beautifully landsaped settings of your hotel.


      

Day-4 :In Kumarakom

Today you are free to relax by the pool or take short optional tours either to the Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary or the Spice Plantations.



Day-5 : Kumarakom -Munaar

You will be driven up to Munaar,preched 1700 metres above the sea level with the view changing from untamed forests to perfectly manicured tea estates


             

Day 6 :In Munaar

Munaar is known as the"Heaven of Planters".Tea garden stretch as far as the eye can see,broken by the occasional splash of waterfalls or quaint villages.Visit some of the vantage points that afford stunning sights or simply relax at your Hotel.


Day-7 : Munaar-Cochin

Transfer back to Cochin for your onward journey back home.
